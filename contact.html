Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you help with bailiffs?

We can provide immediate advice over the phone for emergency situations. For non-emergencies, we typically respond within 2-4 hours during business hours.

Is your advice really free?

Yes, our initial consultation is completely free. We'll assess your situation and explain your options at no cost to you.

What information do I need to provide?

You should have details of any bailiff notices, the amount owed, and the type of debt. If bailiffs are visiting, note the company name and enforcement agent's details.

Can you stop bailiffs immediately?

In many cases, yes. We can often negotiate with bailiffs over the phone to stop immediate enforcement action and arrange more reasonable payment terms.
