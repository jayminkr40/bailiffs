Emergency Bailiff Situation?

If bailiffs are at your door or you have an immediate emergency:

  • Stay calm and do not let them in unless they have a court warrant
  • Ask to see their identification and authorization
  • Call us immediately at 0800 123 4567 for urgent advice
  • Do not make any payments or agreements until you've received advice
